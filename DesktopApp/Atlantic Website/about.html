About Us


Atlantic Industrial Electric Supply Co Ltd provides electrical products and equipment and electrical services to the residential, commercial and industrial sector. With 7 years of experience in the industry, we are dedicated to offering top quality products and unmatched customer service. We profitably and competitively meet or exceed the expectations of our customers. Because we cater to a wide range of industries and clients, we have a diverse customer base in Jamaica. We have an in-house warehouse, allowing us to easily handle all electrical emergency requirements.

We are more than just a distributor. Our team of sales professionals is available to assist you in choosing some of the best product for your needs and your industry. Our certified technicians are reliable to perform any electrical installation work. In our company, we have experts in the electrical business. Customer service is our priority, and we are always happy to lend our expertise to help customers meet their diverse electrical goals of their unique electrical projects.
